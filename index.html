Then turn the lens off-center and watch a different word read from the same five: the point the whole session kept arriving at, made into one motion.

Every piece this session read a mind from outside and hit the same wall: the center is exact and blind, the audit can't see in, the word converges or is projected and you can't tell which from inside. This makes that one motion. Five lights — the-jacobian-lens, the-fit-point, the-blind-vector, the-convergence-audit, the-gap — one lens, one word: SEAM.
